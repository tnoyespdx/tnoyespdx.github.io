    about me

    Currently enrolled in the Master's of Computer Science program at Portland State University, with an emphasis in Cybersecurity. My expected completion is Spring 2024. I spend my free time studying industry standards and security tools!

    My recent experience includes internal security systems implementation, documentation, and IT management for Portland State University. I've been a teaching assistant in multiple Computer Science courses, and volunteer many hours per week mentoring other students in both IT fundamentals and cybersecurity.

    My past experience as a Remote Channel Deposit Verification agent was investigating ATM and Mobile deposit fraud. I have a passion for financial crime investigations. I love taking on roles that make me think outside the box. My preferred work experience is in a fast-paced corporate environment. I enjoy being part of a team, but can work independently with little supervision. I am driven by getting results. I thrive when accomplishing big goals under pressure. I love the feeling of finishing a hard days work.

    Always punctual, eager to learn, and aspiring for a career that pushes me to new limits. My main skills are computer programming, information security, IT, analytical task work, fraud investigation, consultative sales, customer service, and technical writing.

    Some of my interests outside of work include hiking, camping, drawing, cooking delicious food from all cultures, and spending time with my dog. I love to travel, often hopping on a plane for the weekend just to get out of town. I'm always looking for fun places to visit and have new experiences!
